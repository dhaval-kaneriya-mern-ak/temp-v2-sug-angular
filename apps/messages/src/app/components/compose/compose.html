Help: Compose Message

SignUpGenius provides a simple and powerful messaging tool to make communicating with your group members easy. Our "Compose" tab in the Messages area allows you to create a variety of messages.

Invite People to a Sign Up

Select this option to compose an email that will send members a link to your sign up page and invite them to participate. You can send it to an existing group of email addresses in your account or upload a new list of contacts.

Email People Participating in a Sign Up

Select this option to compose an email to people who have already been invited to a sign up. Need to send out directions to your participants? Want to only contact those who haven't signed up and encourage them to get involved? It's easy to send a message to the specific group you need to reach.

Create a Custom Reminder Email Template

With our premium plans, you can compose a custom reminder message that takes place of the standard reminder message. Build a template and assign it to one or more sign ups in your account. You can also add attachments to the email message. Learn More

Create a Custom Confirmation Email Template

A confirmation email is sent to participants immediately after they sign up. With a premium subscription, the sign up creator can customize the confirmation email. You can build a template message and assign it to one or more sign ups in your account. To edit your confirmation template later, look for it in the "Drafts & Templates" tab of the Messages area. Learn More

Invite People to Opt in to Text Messages

Select this option to compose an email that will send members a link to opt in to receive text messages. You can send it to an existing group of email addresses in your account or upload a new list of contacts. Once opted in, you can text participants regarding any sign up in your account. Learn More

Send a Text Message to People Participating in a Sign Up

Select this option to compose a text message. You can send the text to an entire group, only those signed up, individual group members, or even participants signed up for specific sign up slots, including those on a waitlist. If some of the selected recipients have not opted in to text messages, they can receive your message via email. Learn More
